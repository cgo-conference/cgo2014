Hotel/Travel Information

CGO 2014 will be held at the Hyatt Regency Orlando (formerly, The Peabody Hotel)

Hotel registration:

Please use this link to make reservation.

  • Make sure to register by January 17th for the conference rate of $179/night.  We cannot confirm room availability or the CGO rate for reservations confirmed after January 17th.
  • If you prefer to call in your reservation, you can reach the hotel at 1-407-284-1234.
  • The conference rates are valid for stay between Feb.12nd and Feb.19th. If you wish to extend your stay outside of these dates, you will need to book a separate reservation.
  • While the special rates are valid for booking until Jan.17th, it’s strongly recommended that you make reservations at your earliest convenience as the hotel may not have enough rooms for all attendees of the three co-located conferences.

Hotel information:

Hyatt Regency Orlando
9801 International Drive
Orlando, FL 32819, US

Upon arriving at the airport, we recommend two easy ways to get to Hyatt Regency Orlando Hotel. The detailed route can be found in this link.

  • By Mears Shuttle: It is one way and round-trip shuttle transportation from Orlando International Airport to Hyatt Regency Orlando, which departs every 30-40 minutes. Please proceed to Ground Transportation, located one level below baggage claim and look for the Mears Transportation desk. Inform the representative that you need the shuttle to the Hyatt Regency Orlando. You can also choose to book it ahead.
  • By Taxi: non-Mears Taxi Service are available on the Ground Transportation Level on both the A and B sides of the Main Terminal. Approximately $55 one-way.
